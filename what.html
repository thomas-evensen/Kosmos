WELCOME TO KOSMOS!

Kosmos is a relationship visualizer.
In our time as crew7, we developed a bunch of relations. Kosmos tries to take those relations, and visualize them from a three-dee perspective.

Wanna see how close a student felt to the crew? Select the person, and see the dots position themself accoringly.
Wanna see how close the crew was to that person? Turn the table and click on "How do they like me?".

Select a project and see all persons involved

Click on the name to get a detaljed view

Remember to rotate by holding in the left mousebutton and dragging.
